Sg. Siput

Robocon Jr.

A two-day volunteer program at Sungai Siput, Perak introduced Orang Asli children to the world of STEM, focusing on robotics using micro:bits. On the first day, the kids learned the basics of micro:bits and explored how these devices could be programmed. The second day featured a fun competition where the students built robots using micro:bits to pick up sponges and place them in specific locations, all controlled by the students. The program successfully sparked their interest in STEM and empowered them with new skills and confidence.
